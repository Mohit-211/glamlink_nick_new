Glamlink Brand Identity & Design System

  Brand Overview

  Glamlink is a sophisticated beauty and wellness marketplace that bridges technology with personal care services. The brand embodies professional elegance while maintaining
  approachability and innovation.

  Visual Design Philosophy

  - Style: Modern, clean, and minimalist with premium touches
  - Approach: Professional yet approachable, innovative yet trustworthy
  - Aesthetic: Combines luxury beauty industry standards with tech-forward design

  Color Palette

  Primary Colors:
  - Glamlink Teal: #14b8a6 - Primary brand color (vibrant teal/cyan)
  - Glamlink Purple: #a855f7 - Secondary accent (rich purple)
  - Glamlink Gold: #f59e0b - Premium/highlight color (warm gold)

  Gradient Combinations:
  - Teal to Cyan: from-glamlink-teal to-cyan-500
  - Purple to Pink: from-purple-400 to-pink-400
  - Gold to Amber: from-glamlink-gold to-amber-500
  - Multi-color: Teal → Cyan → Blue for dynamic elements

  Supporting Colors:
  - Clean whites and off-whites for backgrounds
  - Soft grays (gray-50, gray-100) for subtle sections
  - Deep grays (gray-700, gray-900) for text

  Icon Design Guidelines

  Style Requirements:
  - Line Weight: 2px stroke (consistent with Lucide icons)
  - Style: Outline/line icons, not filled
  - Corners: Rounded (stroke-linecap="round", stroke-linejoin="round")
  - Complexity: Simple, recognizable shapes with minimal detail
  - Size: Designed for 24x24 viewport, scalable
  - Color: Use currentColor for adaptability

  Visual Characteristics:
  - Clean geometric shapes
  - Subtle animation potential (like radar waves, pulses)
  - Professional but not overly technical
  - Beauty industry appropriate (elegant, refined)
  - Modern tech aesthetic (clean lines, purposeful)

  Animation & Effects

  - Hover States: Subtle scaling (1.02-1.05x), shadow elevation
  - Transitions: Smooth 300-500ms durations
  - Special Effects:
    - Gradient animations for emphasis
    - Pulse effects for activity/live elements
    - Blur effects for depth (blur-xl, blur-2xl)
    - Backdrop blur for overlays

  Typography Approach

  - Headings: Bold, large, confident (often 5xl-7xl)
  - Special Fonts: Playfair Display for magazine/editorial content
  - Body: Clear, readable, professional

  UI Component Patterns

  - Cards: Rounded corners (rounded-2xl), subtle shadows, white backgrounds
  - Buttons: Rounded full or large radius, gradient backgrounds for CTAs
  - Sections: Alternating white/gray-50 backgrounds
  - Overlays: Glass morphism effects with backdrop-blur

  Brand Personality for Icons

  Icons should convey:
  1. Professionalism: Clean, precise, trustworthy
  2. Innovation: Modern, forward-thinking, tech-savvy
  3. Beauty Industry: Elegant, refined, aesthetic
  4. Accessibility: Clear, understandable, universal
  5. Premium Quality: Polished, detailed, thoughtful

  Specific Icon Considerations

  - Beauty tools should be recognizable but simplified
  - Tech features should feel approachable, not intimidating
  - Location/discovery elements should suggest connection and reach
  - Business tools should convey growth and success
  - Social features should feel engaging and community-oriented

  Examples of On-Brand Icon Concepts

  - Mirrors, brushes, and beauty tools with clean lines
  - Interconnected nodes for network/social features
  - Upward trends and growth charts for business tools
  - Map pins with radiating signals for location features
  - Stars and badges for recognition/awards
  - Clean geometric shapes for abstract concepts

  What to Avoid

  - Overly complex or detailed illustrations
  - Sharp, aggressive angles (prefer rounded)
  - Solid filled icons (prefer outlines)
  - Generic tech icons that don't relate to beauty
  - Cluttered or busy designs
  - Inconsistent stroke weights

  This brand identity emphasizes the intersection of beauty expertise and modern technology, creating a premium yet accessible platform for beauty professionals and their clients.
